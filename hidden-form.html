About

The Outreach provides low-income Kent residents and the homeless with food, water, clothing, utility shut-off assistance, drivers license and IDs, and referral infomration. We also provide school supplies and household furnishings, when they are available. Those seeking help must live within the Kent school district, be disabled, have children living with them, or are senior citizens. We always help the homeless.

The Outreach began almost 30 years ago when St. James saw the need arise. During these desparate times, our office is a lifeline for those in need. It has evolved into a place that gives hope to those in need. If you would like to contribute to The Outreach, please (click here).

Services & Requirements

The Outreach offers a variety of services for those who live within the boundaries of the Kent school district.
We support all those who are currently without permanent shelter.

Utility Assistance

Gain assistance with your utilities with an amount going towards your electricity, water or rent.

• Once per calendar year
• Name of applicant/bill match
• Urgent/Shut-off notice required

Gas Voucher

Gain assistance with paying for gas with a voucher.

• Once every six months
• Must have valid/current License
• IDs not accepted

Thrift Store Voucher

Gain access to the Saint James thrift store with a variety of items.

• Once every six months
• Clothing & small household items
• Hours by appointment

Drivers License & Identification Card Assistance

Gain assistance obtaining a drivers license or identification card.

• Check DSHS for Voucher
• After we cover the difference
• We'll meet at the DOL (Directions)

Emergency Goods

Gain assistance with supplies for when you are in need.

• Once per month
• Food & toiletries
